Remembering Steve Jobs

First time I used a Mac it was a PowerPC Quadra. Since that date, I always had an Apple product at my house, and always loved the design and the functionality. The man who coordinated all of this died on 05/11/2011, at 56 years of age. He was such a inspirator for a lot of people, and a genious for the rest. We won't forget you Steve ;)

R.I.P. Steven P. Jobs ~ 1955 - 2011
